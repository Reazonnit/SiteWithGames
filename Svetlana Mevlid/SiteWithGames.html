1.Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 - first-person action RPG developed and published by CD Projekt, known for its popular series of games: The Witcher. The game is an adaptation of the 1988 board game, Cyberpunk 2020. Actions take place fifty-seven years later in the dystopian future. Players will create a custom character named V, and go explore six different regions that unite in the city of Night.Become a cyberpunk - an implanted mercenary - and make a name for yourself on Night City Street.Explore the vast world of Night City, which looks brighter, more complex, and deeper than anything you've seen before.
